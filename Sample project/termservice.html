What Are Cookies?

Cookies are little data files that websites that users visit store on their device. They aid websites with remembering user choices and monitoring user activity to enhance functionality and customisation. Cookies allow websites to keep user sessions over several visits and offer personalised experiences.

What are the Use of Cookies

Cookies facilitate session management, allow for personalised experiences, track user behaviour, and support a number of other features that improve the usability and performance of websites.

How to block Cookies!!

By changing your browser's settings to disable or restrict cookie storage—usually located in the privacy or security settings—you can block cookies. In order to provide users more precise control over cookie management, certain browsers also come with extensions or plugins that let them manage or block cookies specifically for each website.
